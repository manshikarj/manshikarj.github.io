Week 8: Data story

➢Sectors in which Black people work have been hit the hardest

Kudsia Batool at the Bristol Economics Talk Conference highlithed the impact of COVID on Black people, particularly as their sectors were the ones affected by the pandemic the most. This chart shows the percentage of black workers in the various sectors to show the impact of COVID on such minority group. From the visual it is clear that most Black people work as care workers, cleaners, security guards, drivers and nurses: worst hit sectors/jobs by COVID-19 given their nature
